Final Year End Accounts

We prepare year-end accounts for various types of businesses. Trust us to deliver accurate and comprehensive final accounts to keep you in good standing.

We ensure a smooth transition of your final year-end accounts to your accountant. Our comprehensive and organized records make it easy for your accountant to understand your financial position and carry out their tasks efficiently.

About

Katherine Wilde MICB

I am a certified bookkeeper and accounting technician based in Manningtree, Essex, proudly affiliated with the Institute of Certified Bookkeepers.

My qualifications are complemented by my hands-on experience in financial accounting for various types of businesses, including sole traders, limited companies and micro-entities.

Beyond traditional businesses, I also have experience managing the books for clubs and community groups. This breadth of experience enables me to handle unique bookkeeping challenges.

I specialize in Xero, which allows me to provide efficient and expert service. While I have familiarity with other accounting software like QuickBooks, Sage, and Freeagent, my focus is on delivering the best results with Xero.
